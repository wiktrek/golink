GOLINK

Make a link. Share it.
Let it disappear.

A long URL is hard to share and impossible to remember. Golink swaps it for a short one that points to the same place — and can expire when you are done with it.

  1. Paste a long URL. Anything you want to share, however unwieldy.
  2. Get a short link. We give you something like golink/x7Kp2mQa, or pick your own custom ending.
  3. Share it. Anyone who opens the short link is sent straight to your original URL.
  4. Let it expire. Set an expiry date and the link stops working on its own. Leave it blank and it lasts forever.
